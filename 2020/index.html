Important Information

In light of the ongoing situation with the spread of COVID-19, the D Language Foundation and Symmetry Investments have agreed out of an abundance of caution that we should not go forward with DConf 2020. Although it is possible that the situation will have cleared up by June, we can't be certain of that outcome. We do not want to take the chance of endangering the members of the D community. We would prefer to cancel earlier rather than later to minimize the impact on those who have already made travel arrangements.

We know there were some of you who were looking forward to your first DConf, perhaps even your first DConf presentation, and we are terribly sorry you won't have that opportunity this year. But please hold on to that enthusiasm. We plan to be back next year!
